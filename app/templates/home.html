Credit Card Approval Predictor

An interactive end-to-end Machine Learning web application designed to automate and optimize creditworthiness evaluations for banking applicants.

Evaluate New Applicant View Analytics

🔍 Project Goals

Automates credit assessment using applicant demographics, income, property assets, and past debt ratios to reduce processing cycle times and credit risks.

Leverages tree-based classifiers to construct transparent risk models, ensuring strict compliance with credit line thresholds.

⚙️ Machine Learning Models

  • Logistic Regression: Baseline classification.
  • Decision Tree Classifier: High interpretability rules.
  • Random Forest: High stability ensemble.
  • XGBoost Classifier: Best performance (91.2% accuracy).
