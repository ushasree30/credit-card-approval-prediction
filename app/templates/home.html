🔍 Project Goals

Automates credit assessment using applicant demographics, income status, existing EMI commitments, and past default history to optimize processing cycles.

Leverages tree-based classifiers to construct transparent risk models, ensuring strict compliance with credit line thresholds.

⚙️ Evaluation Engines

Runs a hybrid scoring environment consisting of:

  • Rule-Based Pipeline: Strict primary compliance verification.
  • XGBoost Model fallback: ML-based risk assessment (91.2% accuracy).
  • Ensemble Models: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, and Random Forests.
